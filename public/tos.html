REGULATIONS FOR THE USE OF FGPE LEARNING PLATFORM

 

§ 1

Definitions

 

The terms used in the Regulations shall be defined as follows:

1. Service Provider/Personal Data Administrator:

Uniwersytet Szczeciński, al. Papieża Jana Pawła II 22a, 70-453 Szczecin, Poland

2. US – abbreviation for Uniwersytet Szczeciński

3. FGPE Learning Platform – web application created by partners of the consortium of the Framework for Gamified Programming Education project available at https://python.usz.edu.pl/learning-platform/

4. User – individual person, legal person or organisational unit not being a legal person that uses the FGPE Learning Platform.

5. Login – activity of entering identification data allowing to identify the User.

6. Identification Data – login (e-mail address or User’s name) and password established individually by the User in the process of creating a temporary player’s account.

 

§ 2

General Provisions

 

1. These Regulations, herein and after referred to as Regulations describe the rules for using the FGPE Learning Platform.

2. The Regulations mentioned in Item 1 are regulations within the context of Article 8 of the Act of Parliament of 18 July 2002 – Provision of Services by an Electronic Way Law.

3. The FGPE Learning Platform and any educational materials contained in it constitute an exclusive property of its respective authors or the Service Provider unless otherwise specified.

4. The Application may be started up from any web browser.

5. The Services available through FGPE Learning Platform are provided free of charge.

6. The costs of data transmission required to use the FGPE Learning Platform shall be covered by the User on their own under an agreement concluded with the operator of the Internet access service. The Service Provider shall not be responsible for the amount of fees charged for the use of data transmission necessary to use the FGPE Learning Platform.

 

§ 3

The Way the FGPE Learning Platform Operates

 

1. The FGPE Learning Platform allows the User to enjoy the games and programming exercises available in a few language versions.

2. It is possible to log into the FGPE Learning Platform using the selected login and password.

3. In order to register the selected login and password, the User has to provide the Service Provider with the following data: first name, surname, e-mail address, login and password, and to agree to the terms of service as presented here.

 

§ 4

Technical Requirements

 

1. The use of the FGPE Learning Platform requires an access to the Internet network and a device running a web browser.

2. The FGPE Learning Platform has been tested to work properly with the following web browsers: Chrome version 92.0.4515.159, Safari version 14.0.3 (16610.4.3.1.4), and Brave version 1.25.72. The FGPE Learning Platform may work with other web browsers but it has not been tested.

3. The FGPE Learning Platform has been tested to work properly with both mobile devices and desktop computers, however due to the fact that most programming exercises require typing in code, devices with physical keyboard are recommended for the best experience.

 

§ 5

Rules of Using

1. The User shall be obliged to use the FGPE Learning Platform:

    1. In a way consistent with applicable law, these Regulations and the regulations of the browsers with which the FGPE Learning Platform is accessed;
    2. In a way  consistent with the principles of social coexistence, including the general principles of using the Internet network and mobile applications.
    3. In a way that is not inconvenient for other Users and the operator of the Internet access service.
    4. With respect to personal rights of third parties (including the right to privacy) and any other rights they are entitled to.

2. The User shall be obliged to use all information and materials made available through the FGPE Learning Platform only within the scope of permissible use.

3. The User shall be obliged to immediately notify the Service Provider of any infringement of rights in connection with the use of the FGPE Learning Platform.

4. At any time the User may quit using the FGPE Learning Platform. Quitting the use requires closing the FGPE Learning Platform window in the browser.

5. If it has been found that the User performs activities prohibited by law or these Regulations, or violating the principles of social coexistence or prejudicial to the justified interest of the Service Provider, the Service Provider may take actions, including limiting the User's possibility to use the FGPE Learning Platform and the services provided through it.

6. The User shall use the FGPE Learning Platform voluntarily, at their own risk.

2. Using the content published in the FGPE Learning Platform does not mean that the User acquires any copyrights. The User may use this content only to the extent permitted by the Act on Copyright and Neighbouring Rights.

3. No part nor the whole content used by the User may be reproduced and disseminated, in any form or in any other way (electronic or mechanical), including copying and posting on the Internet. The use or enjoyment of the content in whole or in part, without the written consent of its author or the Service Provider, shall be prohibited.

§ 7

Personal Data Protection 

1. Data Administrator:

The Administrator of your personal data is Uniwersytet Szczeciński, al. Papieża Jana Pawła II 22a, 70-453 Szczecin, Poland (herein and after referred to as US).

If you have any queries or doubts, please contact the Data Protection Officer: iod@usz.edu.pl

2. Scope of the Data:

The FGPE Learning Platform processes the following data for every registered User: first name, surname, e‑mail address, login, password, played games, history of submitted exercise solutions, received rewards.

3. Aims to Process Personal Data:

a.      To create an account and send an activation link to the e-mail address provided;

b.      To ensure the proper operation of the FGPE Learning Platform (continuation of learning keeping the progress from the previous sessions of the same user);

c.       Analysis (in order to assess and improve the games and exercises).

4. Legal Basis for Processing Personal Data:

US processes your Data under:
GDPR – Article 6 Section 1 Letter b – Performance of an agreement a party to which is the person concerned.

5. Period of Data Storage:

The Service Provider does not guarantee any minimum data storage period. The User’s data at the FGPE Learning Platform can be erased by the Service Provider at any time due to technical or organisational reasons with no prior notice.

The data belonging to inactive Users will be automatically deleted from the FGPE Learning Platform after 24 months from their last login. 

The data belonging to active Users can be deleted from the FGPE Learning Platform upon the User's request sent to the following address: iod@usz.edu.pl.

6. Disclosure / Transmission of Data:

Information regarding the fact of logging in to the FGPE Learning Platform and the User’s activity therein are not disclosed or transmitted to any third party.

7. Transmission of Data outside the European Economic Area:

The collected data are stored at servers in the premises of Uniwersytet Szczeciński and are not transmitted outside the European Economic Area.

8. Automated Decision Making:

The collected data are not an object of automatic decision making, including profiling.

9. The rights you are entitled to:

In connection with personal data processing you have the right to:

d.      Get an access to the personal data and to receive a copy of them.

e.      Request to rectify the data, if they are incorrect, and to complete the data.

f.        Request to delete the data in case they are not unnecessary to reach the objective for which they have been collected or you will find that they were processed not in compliance with the regulations.

g.       To request restrictions in processing the data (stop all processing operations) in case when you question the accuracy of the data (for the time necessary to check the data) or when you find that your data were processed in a way not in compliance with regulations, but you oppose to have them deleted.

In order to exercise the above rights, please write to the Data Protection Officer of US (iod@usz.edu.pl), who will contact you to process the request.

10. Complaints:

If you find that the processing of personal data by US has violated the provisions of law, you have the right to lodge a complaint to:

President of the Polish Data Protection Commissioner, ul. Stawki 2, 00-193 Warszawa, uodo.gov.pl/pl/p/skargi

The User acknowledges that the FGPE Learning Platform is a computer program, the functioning of which is influenced by a number of factors that require coexistence of the program with other computer programs, computer component drivers, web browsers, other devices, including computer networks or the infrastructure of telecommunications operators.

The User acknowledges that there may be circumstances affecting the operation of the FGPE Learning Platform, that are not within the scope of the Service Provider's responsibility, for which it is not possible to fully examine them and to prevent possible malfunctions in the FGPE Learning Platform.

In connection with the above, any provisions of the Regulations, as well as any element of the content of websites belonging to the Service Provider, should not be read as a guarantee of the proper operation of the FGPE Learning Platform, and the User, despite the lack of such a guarantee, independently decides to start using the FGPE Learning Platform, bearing in mind primarily that the FGPE Learning Platform has not been designed in a way ensuring its optimal functioning on the device owned by the User in the configuration used by them.

The Service Provider may cease the operation of the FGPE Learning Platform at any time due to technical or organisational reasons with no prior notice.

 

§ 9

Complaints procedure

 1. Any complaints regarding the use of the FGPE Learning Platform should be reported within 14 days from the time of finding any irregularities.

2. Reports of complaints shall be made in writing in Polish or English to the Service Provider’s address: Uniwersytet Szczeciński, al. Papieża Jana Pawła II 22a, 70-453 Szczecin, Poland, indicating “FGPE Learning Platform” on the envelope.

3. Report of the complaint should contain: content of the complaint and contact address to which a return information should be sent.

4. Time for considering the complaint and providing the return information is 30 days.

5. Replies to the complaints will be sent by e-mail, traditional mail, fax or telephone, in accordance with the contact details provided by the complainant in the complaint.

 

§ 10

Final Provisions

 1. In matters not covered by these Regulations, the relevant provisions of Polish law shall apply, in particular the Civil Code and the Act of Parliament - Provision of Services by Electronic Way Law.

2. These Regulations are available at the following address: https://python.usz.edu.pl/learning-platform/tos

3. These Regulations become effective from 31.05.2021.
